## üö® Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Menu Complexity
**Issue**: Some menus have 100+ items with complex modifications
**Mitigation**:
- Start with simpler menus (pizza, sandwiches)
- Build robust menu parser
- Allow manual menu curation
- AI learns from corrections

### Risk 2: POS Integration Failures
**Issue**: POS systems can be finicky, APIs change
**Mitigation**:
- Start with email/SMS backup (manual entry)
- Robust error handling
- Fall back to human review if integration fails
- Monitor integration health 24/7

### Risk 3: Voice Quality/Accents
**Issue**: Customers with strong accents may not be understood
**Mitigation**:
- Use best STT models (Whisper excellent with accents)
- Confirmation step (repeat order back)
- Graceful handoff to human if needed
- Multi-language support (Spanish critical)

### Risk 4: Customer Rejection of AI
**Issue**: Some customers don't want to talk to AI
**Mitigation**:
- Make voice VERY human-like (ElevenLabs)
- Option to transfer to human
- Transparent: "Hi, I'm [Restaurant]'s AI assistant"
- Over-deliver on accuracy to build trust

### Risk 5: Competitive Pricing Pressure
**Issue**: Competitors may copy per-order pricing
**Mitigation**:
- Build moat with superior POS integrations
- Faster deployment (5 min setup vs hours)
- Better accuracy through continuous learning
- Bundle with other AgentFlow services
