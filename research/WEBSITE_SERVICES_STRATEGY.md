## üö® Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: AI-Generated Sites Look Generic
**Issue**: All sites look the same, customers unhappy
**Mitigation**:
- 5+ diverse templates per industry
- Customization options (colors, fonts, layouts)
- Manual override for advanced users
- Professional design review before template launch

### Risk 2: Widget Compatibility Issues
**Issue**: Widget breaks on certain sites/themes
**Mitigation**:
- Extensive testing on popular platforms
- Fallback to iframe if script fails
- Clear documentation
- Fast support response for issues

### Risk 3: Hosting Costs Explode at Scale
**Issue**: Bandwidth/hosting becomes expensive
**Mitigation**:
- Aggressive image optimization
- CDN caching
- Static site generation (cheap to host)
- Monitor per-customer costs, adjust pricing if needed

**Issue**: Customers use all 5 updates every month
**Mitigation**:
- Upsell to priority support ($10/month for unlimited)
